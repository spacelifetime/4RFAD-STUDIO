üéØ Quick Start with Netlify

This is the fastest way to get your site online:

  1. Open netlify.com
  2. Click "Sign up" and create an account
  3. On the dashboard, look for "Drag and drop your site output folder here"
  4. Drag your entire website folder (the one containing index.html) into that area
  5. Wait a few seconds for deployment
  6. Your site will be live with a URL like: random-name.netlify.app
  7. You can customize the URL in the site settings

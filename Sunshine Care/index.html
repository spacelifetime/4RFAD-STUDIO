Home Health Care Experts Serving Central Minnesota

Sunshine Care Services Inc. is a family-owned and operated business proudly serving the communities of St. Paul, St. Cloud, Coon Rapids, and Minneapolis, MN. We offer personalized home health care services designed to keep our clients safe and comfortable.

Our company is well-regarded across the region for our comprehensive services that enhance the quality of life for individuals in their own homes. We are committed to providing compassionate and high-quality care, allowing our clients to live their daily lives with dignity and respect.

Our Compassionate Services for Clients Central Minnesota

Sunshine Care performs client home care services with the utmost concern for their security and comfort.

Personal Care Assistance (PCA)

Provides support with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, eating, and mobility. PCAs assist individuals who need help due to age, illness, or disability, ensuring they can maintain their independence and quality of life.

Home Making

This service includes assistance with household tasks such as cleaning, laundry, meal preparation, and shopping. It helps individuals maintain a clean, safe, and healthy living environment.

Individualized Home Supports With Training

This service offers support and training to individuals with disabilities to help them develop and improve skills needed for independent living. The focus is on teaching skills related to daily living, community integration, and maintaining a safe and healthy lifestyle.
